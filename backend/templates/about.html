Mauritian Recipe Finder

Case-Based Reasoning powers our recipe suggestions. We map your inputs to a food ontology, score recipes by overlap with your pantry, and adapt them with smart substitutions when something is missing or must be avoided.

  • Ingredient normalization (supports local names & synonyms)
  • Allergen & diet filtering
  • Transparent change logs for substitutions

FAQ

How are substitutions chosen?
We combine explicit rules and same-parent fallbacks in the food ontology, then filter for diet and allergens. Each change lists a reason and a score.
Why do some ingredient names look technical?
Ingredient IDs come from a food ontology. We attach friendly labels where possible; occasionally you may see the ID if a label isn’t available yet.
Can I save or share recipes?
Yes — open a recipe and use Share or Print. Saving to favorites is included as a demo; persistent accounts are planned.
